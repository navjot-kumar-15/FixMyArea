# Project Description

**CivicConnect** is a platform where citizens can report local public issues in their area and track their resolution process.

The goal is to connect:

**Citizens ↔ Local Authorities ↔ Workers**

Instead of complaints being scattered across WhatsApp groups, social media posts, or ignored completely, the system creates a structured and trackable workflow.

Examples of issues:

- Road potholes
- Street light failures
- Water leakage
- Garbage collection problems
- Sewage issues
- Broken traffic signals
- Illegal dumping
- Public safety concerns
- Internet or utility disruptions

---

# Why Build This?

Most people experience this situation:

```text
Person notices road damage

↓

Tells friends

↓

Posts on social media

↓

Nothing happens
```

Problems:

- No centralized system
- Duplicate complaints
- No visibility into progress
- Authorities receive scattered information
- Citizens do not know whether someone already reported the issue

The platform solves this:

```text
Person notices issue

↓

Creates report

↓

Nearby users support it

↓

Issue becomes visible

↓

Authority reviews

↓

Worker assigned

↓

Status updates shown

↓

Issue resolved
```

---

# Real Problem Statement

Imagine this:

A water pipeline breaks near a school.

Current scenario:

- 100 people see it
- 40 people complain separately
- Nobody knows if it was reported
- Days pass

With CivicConnect:

```text
1 report created

↓

People support existing report

↓

Priority increases

↓

Authority notices higher urgency

↓

Worker resolves issue

↓

Everyone gets notified
```

# How This Can Help India

India has large populations and local issues are often reported informally.

Examples:

- Roads damaged after rain
- Garbage collection delays
- Water leakage
- Broken street lights
- Illegal waste dumping

Challenges:

- Slow communication
- Repeated complaints
- Lack of transparency
- Difficulty prioritizing issues

CivicConnect can help by:

### 1. Creating Transparency

Citizens see:

```text
Status:
Under Review
Assigned
Resolved
```

instead of uncertainty.

---

### 2. Reducing Duplicate Complaints

Instead of:

```text
500 complaints
```

System creates:

```text
1 issue
500 supporters
```

---

### 3. Data-driven Decision Making

Authorities can discover:

```text
Area A:

Road complaints: 320
Water complaints: 42
Electricity complaints: 10
```

Patterns become visible.

---

### 4. Faster Prioritization

Urgent issues rise automatically:

```text
Severity score:

Votes
+
Affected people
+
Time unresolved
```

---

### 5. Long-term Smart City Support

Over time the collected data could help:

- Smart city planning
- Budget allocation
- Infrastructure improvements
- Predictive maintenance
